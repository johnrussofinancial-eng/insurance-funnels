The Problem

What Most Federal Employees
Never Find Out Until It's Too Late

01

FEGLI Is Overpriced After 50

Federal group life insurance costs skyrocket after age 50. Most employees don't realize private coverage can be 40–60% cheaper — with better benefits.

02

TSP Left on Autopilot

The default TSP allocation is rarely optimal for your retirement date. Without a withdrawal strategy, you could pay significantly more in taxes than necessary.

03

The Pension Gap at Death

If you die before retirement, your family may receive very little from FERS. Life insurance bridges that gap — but most federal employees are underinsured or overpaying.

04

FEHB Cost Shock in Retirement

Healthcare premiums don't go away when you retire. Without planning, FEHB costs can consume a significant portion of your pension income.

05

Early Retirement Penalty

Leaving before your MRA can permanently reduce your pension. We help you model the true cost of going early — and strategies to offset it.

06

No Supplemental Income Plan

Your pension replaces a portion of your salary — not all of it. Most federal retirees face an income gap. An IUL or annuity strategy can fill it tax-efficiently.

Common Questions

Is this review really free?
Yes, completely. There's no charge for the consultation and no obligation to purchase anything. We believe in earning your trust before asking for your business.
Are you affiliated with the government or OPM?
No. We are independent, licensed insurance and financial professionals. We are not affiliated with any government agency, OPM, or federal employer. We work for you.
I'm 15 years from retirement — is it too early to do a review?
Actually, the earlier the better. The most impactful changes — like building supplemental income through an IUL or reducing FEGLI costs — work best when started early. Waiting until you're 2 years out limits your options significantly.
What if I'm already close to retirement?
We work with federal employees at every stage, including those retiring within 1–2 years. There are still meaningful optimizations available around TSP withdrawal strategy, SBP decisions, and FEHB coverage that can significantly impact your retirement income.
What products do you offer?
We offer life insurance (term and permanent), Indexed Universal Life (IUL) policies, and annuities — all selected independently from 40+ carriers. Every recommendation is based on what's best for your situation, not what pays us the most commission.
Do I need to have my benefits paperwork ready for the call?
Not necessarily. When you book, we'll send a short prep guide with helpful items to have on hand. But even without documents, we can give you a solid overview and identify your likely gaps in the first call.
