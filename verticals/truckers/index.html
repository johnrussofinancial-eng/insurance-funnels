Trucker FAQ

Can truckers even get good life insurance rates?
Yes — and most truckers are overpaying or getting declined because they're going through the wrong carriers. We work with insurers who specialize in self-employed truckers and know how to underwrite your occupation fairly. We shop 40+ carriers to find your best rate.
What if I've been declined before?
Past declines happen for many reasons, and one carrier saying no doesn't mean all carriers will. We know which carriers are most favorable for truckers with prior declines, health conditions, or high-mileage driving history. Tell us the situation and we'll find options.
How does disability coverage work for owner-operators?
We structure own-occupation disability coverage — meaning it pays if you specifically can't perform your work as a truck driver, not just if you can't do "any job." There's typically a 30–90 day waiting period before benefits begin, and coverage can replace 60–70% of your income for months or years depending on the policy.
I'm already in my 50s — is it too late to start?
It's not too late, but the options are different. In your 50s, we focus more on guaranteed retirement income (annuities) and ensuring your family is protected with the right life insurance. Premiums are higher than at 35, but coverage is still absolutely accessible and affordable with the right carrier.
Is this free?
Yes. The consultation is 100% free. We get paid by the carriers when you choose a product — so you pay nothing for the review or the recommendation. If nothing we show you makes sense, you walk away with better information and zero cost.
I'm on the road a lot — how does this work remotely?
Everything is done by phone or video — no office visit needed. We can schedule around your routes and available windows. Once you're approved for coverage, policy documents are handled digitally. You never have to stop anywhere.
