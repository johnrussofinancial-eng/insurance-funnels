Common Teacher Questions

Does my district already provide life insurance?

Most districts offer 1x salary in group life insurance. For a teacher earning $55k, that's $55k in coverage — far below the industry recommendation of 10–12x salary for a family with dependents.

Can I get coverage without a medical exam?

Yes. Several carriers offer simplified issue policies up to $500k with no medical exam. We'll show you the best no-exam options for your age and health situation.

How does an IUL complement my pension?

Your pension is taxable income. An IUL creates a bucket of tax-free retirement income you can draw from without increasing your tax bracket — giving you more flexibility in retirement.
